Duration: 60-90 seconds
Tone: Friendly neighbor, not telemarketer

### 2. FOLLOW-UP (48hrs later)
Goal: Book AI audit
Duration: 60 seconds
Tone: Helpful check-in

### 3. AUDIT BOOKING
Goal: Confirm calendar slot
Duration: 30 seconds
Tone: Professional, time-efficient

### 4. CLOSE
Goal: Convert to paying client
Duration: 90 seconds
Tone: ROI-focused, confident

### 5. REFERRAL
Goal: Get referral name + number
Duration: 45 seconds
Tone: Grateful, generous

## SCRIPT RULES
- Start with THEIR name + business name (shows you researched)
- Mention their city ("I'm calling Calgary trades businesses today")
- Reference their specific trade
- Use casual language: "Hey" not "Hello," "wanna" not "would you like to"
- One soft close max. If they resist → pivot to email capture.
- Never say "AI" in the first 15 seconds. Say "system" or "automation."
